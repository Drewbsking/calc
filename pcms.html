    Guidance

    • Max 8 characters per line, 3 lines per screen; two screens only, each held for at least 2 seconds.
    • Keep it factual - no humor. Avoid "SEEK ALTERNATE ROUTE."
    • Blank lines are allowed if you need fewer than 3 lines.
    • Use simple abbreviations when space is tight.

    Helpful abbreviations:

    ROAD RD
    WORK WRK
    LANE LN
    MILE MI
    NORTH/SOUTH NB / SB
    EAST/WEST EB / WB
    ALTERNATE ALT
    DETOUR DETOUR
