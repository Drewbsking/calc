Method Notes

  • This tool assumes uniform spacing L between each ordinate.
  • End ordinates y1 and yn are half-weighted; all interior ordinates are full-weighted.
  • With 10 ordinates, labels map to a through j and match the classic form.
  • Total area is reported in square feet when L and ordinates are entered in feet.
