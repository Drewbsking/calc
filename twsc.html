Two-Way Stop-Controlled (TWSC) Calculator

Follows Chapter 32 Example 1 from the 2022 HCM: three-leg TWSC with a stop-controlled minor approach.

Analysis settings

Grade and heavy vehicles feed into the critical/follow-up headway adjustments. Use the heavy-vehicle percent for the approach you are analyzing (major LT uses major %, minor LT/RT use minor %). Use the grade on that same approach; set to 0 for level.

Volumes

Enter either peak 15-min counts or hourly flows. Choose below and the tool will handle the conversion.

EB = eastbound major, WB = westbound major, NB = stop-controlled minor. Enter counts for your analysis approach.
